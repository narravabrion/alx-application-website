Welcome to my tech life...

Mission

Hi there, my name is Brion, former C.E.O of a lemonade stand. If you can see and enjoy this page then you have technology to thank for that. Technology has made a lot of impossibilities possible. We can now solve a lot of our daily communal problems with ese using the power of technology. Every year the world population keeps on increasing and thus putting pressure on our food supply. We need food to survive and I see an opportunity in using Technology to make argriculture more efficient and increase output. With smart argriculture it is possible to ensure a constant food suppy for current and future generations especially in Africa without adversely affecting the environment.
I believe ALX will provide me with the opportunity to get the right skills and meet like minded individuals that'll enable me to fulfill my dreams. I can also leverage the community provided by ALX to build a network that'll help grow my career as a software developer.

Opportunity

The biggest challenge for those willing to learn software development especially in Africa and other third world countries is the lack of learning resources. Internet connectivity, computers or laptops are viewed as a luxury and sometimes too expensive to come by. It would be good to open a resource center where curious minds can be taught about programming and hence enable them realise their dream.

Venture

As a first year at Jomo Kenyatta University of argriculture and Technology, I creadted a platform that could be used to track lost documents. Basically, users could either post lost documents they recovered or browse the site for documents they lost. This would enable people to avoid the long process involved in replacing the lost document.

Ambitions

The most audacious dream I have is to created an integrated population database for people in Africa that would enable citizens of any African state to move freely across Africa without the need for permits or Visas.
This would promote a cohesive continent, promote business and reduce poverty by eliminating punitive taxes and other barriers that make it expensive to start and run a business now.
